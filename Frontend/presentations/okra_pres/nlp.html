Topic extraction of train company reviews: a model comparison

By James Twose

Problem Statement and Innovation

Goal

  • Extract topics that are discussed in reviews of train companies to inform client strategy towards customer experience

Challenges

  • Imbalance in reviews per company
  • Review text data requires in depth preprocessing
  • Over representation of certain parts of the population

Approach

  • Import and clean data

  • Audition topic extraction methods (spacy, BERTopic, gensim, sklearn)

  • Visualize topics per method

What does this mean for us?


    • No matter what topic model we use it seems that the main topics of interest are:
      • the customer service
      • the train service itself (delays, strikes, running on time etc.)
      • the state of the trains (cleanliness, etc.)
      • the ease of use of the website
      • in general the ease of booking a journey
    • low star reviews:
      • poor customer service
      • the train service - delays and strikes
    • high star reviews:
      • kind customer service
      • state of the trains

So what's next?

  • Explore other more complex text extraction models
  • Further tune the preprocessing steps
  • Include text summarization models in the preprocessing
  • Create interactive visualizations
